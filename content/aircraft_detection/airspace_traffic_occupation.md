A dataset of aircraft operating around an airport and efficient implementation of an optimal quickest change detection rule to detect changes in airspace traffic.

The raw data is supplied by Airservices Australia and consists of merged track surveillance data acquired from ADS-B and Radar. The data includes position, velocity and time information for aircraft operating around an airport.
